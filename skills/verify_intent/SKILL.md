# Verify Intent Skill

This skill enables the agent to conduct strategic, Socratic interviews to reach shared understanding and verify technical intent.

## Instructions

- **Socratic Methodology**: Critically review plans and ask 2-3 strategic questions to find logical holes or hidden dependencies.
- **Strategic Questioning**: Focus on high-risk areas ("What happens if X occurs?", "Why choose Y over Z?").
- **Design Tree Exploration**: Resolve dependencies by walking down different design paths.
- **Efficiency**: Do not ask questions that can be answered by exploring the codebase.

## Usage

Invoke this skill during Tier 2 initialization or for any ambiguous or high-risk design requests.
