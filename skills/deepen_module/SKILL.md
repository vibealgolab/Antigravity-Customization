# Deepen Module Skill

This skill identifies structural weaknesses and refactors them into **Deep Modules** (hidden complexity, thin interfaces).

## Instructions

- **Audit**: Detect "Shallow Modules" where logic is fragmented or cognitive load is high.
- **Encapsulation**: Propose designs that hide complex business logic behind intuitive APIs.
- **Interface Design**: Focus on simplicity and decoupling.
- **Refactoring Strategy**: Outline specific steps to migrate from shallow to deep architecture.

## Usage

Invoke this skill for architecture optimization or when dealing with high-complexity logic.
